Vinder – Peer-to-Peer Farmers Market

UX case study of a peer-to-peer marketplace enabling local producers to deliver fresh food directly to consumers. Complete design system including personas, journey maps, and blueprints.

Overview

Understanding the challenge and defining project scope

The goal was to create a seamless marketplace experience for local farmers and consumers. Research identified trust, logistics, and payment simplicity as the core UX challenges.

My role included user research, information architecture, UI design, and interaction prototyping using Figma.

UX Process

From insights to interaction design

  • Research: Interviews with farmers and consumers to map trust and delivery pain points.
  • Personas: Created 9 personas representing local producers, logistics partners, and customers.
  • Wireframes: Built flow prototypes for marketplace listing and checkout experience.
  • Service Blueprint: Modeled both B2B and B2C touchpoints for scalability.
