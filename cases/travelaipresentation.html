Principles that guide this work

Architecture is product. System design directly affects trust, explainability, and viability.

Transparency over automation. AI should parse, not decide. Logic must be traceable and overridable.

Constraints enable clarity. A single orchestration service and tight scope prevent complexity sprawl.

This is not a booking engine. It is a system for understanding, structuring, and orchestrating travel intent.
