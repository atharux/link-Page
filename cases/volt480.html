Case Study: Mobile Prototype

Design and deliver a clickable mobile prototype based on a service web platform

Client: Volt480
I worked remotely with two other people: CEO and Head of Product

Challenge: Deliver new mobile user journey for the sales team to demonstrate the service at a trade show.

First, I met with the CEO and Product Manager to understand the purpose of the app and simplify user journeys, so I could begin wireframing the main flows.

After studying the process, I decided to use a tabbed sticky menu with a progress bar as a visual signal. This let the user know exactly where they were in the multi-step process.

Iterative process

With main steps established I began experimenting with streamlining process, adding feedback, and signifiers

Once I verified the UX, I updated the UI layer in Marvel and then created the Zeplin project to communicate specs with the devs.

My contributions to the project

Journeys
Multistep tabbed and scroll navigation
Flows
Progress feedback
Mobile

Conclusion

I delivered a Marvel prototype along with a Zeplin project with final UI within 2 weeks. VOLT480 was able to successfully demonstrate the product on a mobile device at the industry trade show.
